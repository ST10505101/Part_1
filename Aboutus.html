About Swanked

Founded in 2013 by Lindelani Nedoboni, Swanked is a South African streetwear and sneaker marketplace. What started as a passion project has grown into a popular curated platform for sneaker enthusiasts and street culture.

Mission

Swanked aims to provide a meticulously curated inventory of authentic streetwear whilst staying at the forefront of South African street culture and trends.

Target Audience

Gen Z, Millennials, sneaker collectors, and urban fashion enthusiasts.
